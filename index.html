About

I am Neetesh Pandey, a Postdoctoral Research Scientist at Columbia University, New York, USA. My research targets Alzheimer’s disease genetics through multi-phenotype rare-variant analyses (e.g., SKAT-family / Multi- & Meta-MultiSKAT), biomarker modeling (ptau and complementary plasma markers), and population-aware genomics. I integrate GWAS, single-cell and epigenomic readouts, and multi-omics priors to connect association signals to regulatory and gene-level mechanisms.

I have authored 28 peer-reviewed publications and presented at leading international conferences including AAIC and ADPD. Current interests include cross-ancestry inference, fine-mapping of rare signals, and mechanism-anchored variant prioritization to accelerate translation from statistical signal to biological function.
