About

I am Neetesh Pandey, a Postdoctoral Research Scientist at Columbia University, New York, USA. My research focuses on Alzheimer’s disease genetics, multi-phenotype rare variant analysis, biomarker prediction, and population-based genomics. I integrate statistical genetics, computational biology, and functional genomics to unravel mechanisms underlying neurological and neurodegenerative disorders.

I have authored 28 peer-reviewed publications and presented at leading international conferences including AAIC and ADPD. With expertise spanning GWAS, single-cell omics, multi-omics integration, and computational drug design, my work bridges statistical discoveries to biological mechanisms, ultimately contributing to improved risk prediction and therapeutic insights for Alzheimer’s disease.
